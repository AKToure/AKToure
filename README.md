# About

I am an engineering student at the **Université de Technologie de Troyes (UTT)** and the founder of **Alpha Stochastic Research**, an independent quantitative finance research laboratory.

My research lies at the intersection of

- Quantitative Finance
- Stochastic Processes
- Financial Mathematics
- Portfolio Optimization
- Quantitative Risk Management
- Financial Machine Learning
- Bayesian Statistics
- Scientific Computing
- Artificial Intelligence
- Open Science

My objective is to build reproducible quantitative methodologies that bridge rigorous mathematics with real-world financial decision-making.

# Research Philosophy

<div align="center">

## Market → Model → Insight → Decision

</div>

| Stage | Description |
|:------|:------------|
| **Market** | Acquire, clean and structure financial data |
| **Model** | Develop mathematical, statistical and stochastic models |
| **Insight** | Quantify uncertainty, detect alpha and estimate risk |
| **Decision** | Produce systematic investment and risk-management strategies |
