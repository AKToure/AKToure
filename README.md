## 🔭 Current Research

**Core focus areas:** `Portfolio Optimization` `Bayesian Allocation` `Financial Machine Learning` `Quantitative Risk Modeling` `Market Microstructure`

<br>

**Portfolio Optimization Engine**
`Code · Private Alpha`
Mean-variance and risk-parity allocation framework with convex-optimization backends (CVXPY), covariance shrinkage estimators, and constrained rebalancing. Benchmarked against classical MPT baselines.

**Financial Machine Learning Models**
`Code · Private Alpha`
Supervised and probabilistic models for return prediction and regime detection, built on a PyTorch/JAX research stack with a focus on out-of-sample robustness over backtest overfitting.

**Modern Portfolio Theory, Revisited**
`Paper · Alpha Stochastic Research · Drafting`
Re-examines classical mean-variance assumptions under non-Gaussian return distributions and estimation-error uncertainty, proposing robust reformulations grounded in modern convex optimization.

**Hidden Markov Models in Finance**
`Article · Substack · Active Research`
Applies HMMs to detect latent market regimes from price and volatility series, with an emphasis on transparent, reproducible derivations rather than black-box signals.

**Bayesian Portfolio Allocation**
`Article · Medium · Planned`
A practitioner-oriented walkthrough of Bayesian shrinkage and Black-Litterman-style allocation, connecting prior beliefs to posterior-optimal portfolios.
